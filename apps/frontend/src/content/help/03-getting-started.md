## Signing in

Open the app and sign in with the username and password your administrator created.
If two-factor authentication (2FA) is enabled on your account you'll be prompted for
a 6-digit code after your password. Single sign-on (OIDC) buttons appear on the
login page if your administrator configured a provider.

## The layout

- **Dashboard** — the live status of every monitored host at a glance.
- **Sidebar** — navigate to agents, logs, uptime, reports, wallboards, admin, docs
  and more (you only see what your role permits).
- **Ticker** — a scrolling announcement bar at the bottom for important notices.
- **Help** — the public Documentation (this site) and, for developers, the
  Developer docs.

> If a page or button is missing, your role doesn't grant it — access comes from
> your **groups → roles → permissions**. Ask an administrator.
